Description

Sensodyne is the top toothpaste brand across the globe that treats dental sensitivity. With Sensodyne toothpaste, people suffering from sensitive teeth can not only find relief but also enjoy daily activities. This makes Sensodyne the most recommended toothpaste by dentists for patients suffering from dental sensitivity. Its specially designed formula is clinically proven to provide all-day protection from sensitivity, making the toothpaste a favourite among 9 out of 10 doctors surveyed across the United States. Sensodyne Fresh Mint provides a fresh minty aftertaste for a clean and energetic feeling without any pain or sensitivity in teeth or gums.

Benefits

Sensodyne Fresh Mint toothpaste gives 24-hour protection and relief from the sensitivity of the oral cavity.

Its specially designed formulation allows long-lasting protection from sensitivity.

It gives complete protection from cavities.

Sensodyne Fresh Mint toothpaste offers a fresh and clean aftertaste as an added benefit.

Ingredients

Potassium nitrate, Sodium fluoride, Sodium hydroxide, Pentasodium triphosphate, Sodium methyl cocoyl taurate, Hydrated silica, Sorbitol, PEG-8, Titanium dioxide, Cocamidopropyl betaine, Xanthan gum, flavour, sodium saccharin, sucralose, glycerin, water.

Uses

Sensodyne toothpaste is used to give relief to patients suffering from sensitive teeth. The fresh minty flavour also aids in keeping the oral cavity feeling fresh. The toothpaste boasts of its unique formula that gives prolonged protection against sensitivity and prevents tooth decay. It also helps to strengthen teeth and prevent disorders of the gum and foul breath.

How to Use

Sensodyne must be used twice a day but not more than three times a day. Apply an approximately 1-inch strip of the toothpaste on a toothbrush with soft bristles and brush for at least 1 minute. Replace the lid of the tube tightly to keep the contents dry. After using the toothpaste, spit out the contents. Do not swallow after brushing. This product is recommended for adults and children above 12 years of age.

Sensodyne is intended for oral use only.

FAQ

1. Is Sensodyne toothpaste effective?

Tooth sensitivity is a serious problem that requires urgent attention from dentists. Sensodyne must be used according to the advice of dentists to receive the best benefits and protection from sensitivity. In case the problem persists or worsens or remains the same even after 4 weeks, seek medical advice.

2. Can I develop allergies from using Sensodyne?

It is unlikely to develop any allergies from using Sensodyne. However, if you face any irritation stop using the toothpaste and consult a physician.

3. Can I develop cavities even after using the product?

Sensodyne is an integral part of daily dental care at home and helps protect from cavities. However, one may still develop cavities from other factors such as lifestyle habits, food consumption, the effectiveness of brushing, etc.

4. Can children use Sensodyne toothpaste?

For children younger than 12 years of age, use it as recommended by a dentist or doctor.
